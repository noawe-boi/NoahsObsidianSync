

### [[Weight]] = M x G

#### Where:
#####
- M = [[Mass]] in KG
- G = the strength of gravitational field
- Weight = [[Force]] Force in newtons 



### Examples

#### The [[Weight]] of a 1kg object is
##### Weight = mg = 1kg x 9.8N/kg = 9.8N

#### The weight of a 2500kg object is
##### Weight = mg = 2500kg x 9.8N/kg = 24500N
