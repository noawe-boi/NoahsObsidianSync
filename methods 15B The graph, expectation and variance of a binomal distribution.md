### Foundational Concepts
#### Expected values
For a given trial for a variable, we have 
$$E(x)=P$$
$$\textrm{Var}(x)=1-p$$

### Big ideas and concepts
#### Number of successes
If $x$ is the number of success in $n$ trials each with probability of $p$ then we have 
$E(x)=np$
$Var(x)=np(1-p)$
