## Consider $y=ln(x)$
$$ x=e^y \: \: \rightarrow \: \: \frac{dy}{dx} = e^y \: \: \rightarrow \: \: \frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{e^{ln(x)}} = \frac{1}{x}$$

i.e
$$\frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{\frac{d}{dx}(x)}{x}$$
or $$\frac{d}{dx}​\ln (f(x))=\frac{f(x)}{f′(x)​}$$
