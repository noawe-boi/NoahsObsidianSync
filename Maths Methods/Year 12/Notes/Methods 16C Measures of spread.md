#### Standard deviation
$\sigma=\sqrt{\textrm{Var}(X)}$
#### IQR of $x$
$b-a$, such that 
$\int ^a _{-\infty} f(x) dx = 0.25 \&\int^b_{-\infty}f(x) dx = 0.75$
$Pr(\mu -2\sigma \leq X \leq \mu + 2 \sigma)\approx0.95$
