## Anti differentiation of power functions
### Foundational Concepts

#### Differentiation
if $y=x^n$, $\frac{dy}{dx} = nx^{x-1}$ 

#### Anti-Differentiation 
if $\frac{dy}{dx} = x^n, y=\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} +c$,
