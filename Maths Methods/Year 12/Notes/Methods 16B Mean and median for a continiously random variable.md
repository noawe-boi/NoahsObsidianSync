#### Median

### Big ideas and concepts
#### Probability and graph
Remember that probability equates to area under the graph

#### Percentile
For the $n^{th}$ percentile, $\frac{n}{100}$ of the area sits under the graph 
i.e. $\int ^p _{-\infty}f(x)=\frac{n}{100}$
