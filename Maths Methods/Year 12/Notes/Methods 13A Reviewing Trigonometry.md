### Foundational concepts
#### SOHCAHTOA
![[Right angle triangle]]

- $\sin(\theta)=\frac{O}{H}$
- $\cos(\theta)=\frac{A}{H}$
- $\tan(\theta)=\frac{O}{A}$


### Big ideas and concepts
$$\frac{\sin(\theta)}{cos(\theta)}=\frac{\frac{O}{H}}{\frac{A}{H}}$$
$$=tan(\theta)$$
