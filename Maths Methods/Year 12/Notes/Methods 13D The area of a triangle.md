### Big ideas and concepts

#### Area of a triangle

##### Finding $h$
$\sin(A)=\frac{h}{c}$
$\therefore h=c\sin(A)$
##### Finding area
$\textrm{Area}=\frac{1}{2}bh$
$=\frac{1}{2}bc\sin(A)$
#### Vocab and definitions
$$Area = \frac{1}{2}bc\sin(A)$$
