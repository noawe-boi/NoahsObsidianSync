Angular momentum, L
Given by
$L =mvr$ 


$mvr=\frac{nh}{2\pi}$
Where
$n=$ Energy level of the electron 
$h=$ Plank's Constant
<div style="font-size:10">
Note this implies 'quantized' or discrete momentum levels. 
i.e. Its integer like where there are only certain values it can be
</div>
