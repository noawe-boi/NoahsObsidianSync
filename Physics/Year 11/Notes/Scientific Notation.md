#physics 
## Rules
- Must have 1 non zero digit before the decimal place
- Multiply by a power of 10 (1x10^1)



## Examples

- 764000 = 7.64x10^5
- 0.000062 = 6.2x10^-5
- 400000m (3 significant digits) = 4.00x10^5 m = 4.00x10^2 km
