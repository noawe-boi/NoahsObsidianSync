## What is acceleration
Acceleration is a measure of change in velocity over time


## Measuring acceleration
### Acceleration
$a=\frac{v-u}{t}$ 
- a=acceleration
- u=initial speed
- t=time
- v=velocity

or

$v=u+at$ 
### Change in velocity
$\Delta V = V_{Final} - V_{Inital}$

or 

$\Delta V = V - u$
