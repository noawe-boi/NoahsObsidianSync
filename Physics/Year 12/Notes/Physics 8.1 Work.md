## Definition
The energy given to or done by a system

### Formula
$$W = Fs$$ Where
- $F = \textrm{force} \space (N)$
- $W = \textrm{work} \space (J)$ or sometimes $(Nm)$ or newton meters
- $s = \textrm{displacement} \space (s)$
