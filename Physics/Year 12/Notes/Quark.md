### What is a quark?
A fundamental particle
#### Anti Quarks
The [[Antimatter]] equivalent of a quark. 
- Same mass, opposite charge.
- Opposite Color
- Just add "Anti" before the name.
- Put a bar above regular symbol (e.g. $\bar{u}$ for anti-up)
