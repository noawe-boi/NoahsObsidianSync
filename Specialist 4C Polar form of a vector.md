#### Formulas
$$\vec{v}[r, \theta, \alpha]$$

$$\vec{v} = x\hat{j} + y{\hat{j}} + z\hat{j}$$

$$x=r\cos\theta \cos \alpha$$
$$y=r\sin \theta \cos \alpha$$
$$z=r\sin\alpha$$
$$r=\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 _+z^2}$$

$$\tan \theta = \frac{y}{x}$$
$$\sin \alpha =\frac{z}{r}$$
