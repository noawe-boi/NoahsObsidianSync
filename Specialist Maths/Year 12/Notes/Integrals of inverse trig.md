#### Integrals of inverse trig
##### $\sin^{-1}$
$\int\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}\right)dx=\sin^{-1}(\frac{x}{a})+c$
##### $\cos^{-1}$
$\int\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2+x^2}}\right)dx=\cos^{-1}(\frac{x}{a})+c$
##### $\tan^{-1}$
$\int\left(\frac{a}{{a^2+x^2}}\right)dx=\tan^{-1}(\frac{x}{a})+c$
