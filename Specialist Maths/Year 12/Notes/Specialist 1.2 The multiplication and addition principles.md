## Foundational Concepts
### Probability
To find the probability of event $E$ occurring:
$Pr(E) = \frac{n(E)}{n(\textrm{Total})}$

### The addition principle
To find the probability of $AB$ or $AB'$ occuring:
$Pr(AB \cup AB') = Pr(AB) + Pr(AB')$
