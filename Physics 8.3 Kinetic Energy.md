## What is Kinetic Energy
The energy associated with motion. 

### Finding Kinetic Energy
Remember that $W=Fs$, 
Clearly then $W=ma(\frac{v^2-u^2}{2a}) \: \: \rightarrow \: \: W=\frac{1}{2}m(v^2 - u^2)$
$$E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$
