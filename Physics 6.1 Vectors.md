##### Textbook Example 6.1.3
Determine the change in velocity of an aero plane as it changes from 255m/s west to 160m/s east using sign and directions conventions
$\Delta V = V_f - V_i$
$= 160 - -255$
= $+415$
or $415ms^-1$ east
