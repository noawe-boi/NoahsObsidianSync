### Differentiateing sin, cos and tan
#### Sin
$$\frac{d}{dx} (sin(x)) = cos(x)$$

#### Cos
$$\frac{d}{dx} (cos(x)) = -sin(x)$$


#### Tan
we dont talk about this for now


### For functions with functions inside 
#### Sin
$$\frac{d}{dx} (sin[f(x)]) = f'(x)cos[f(x)]$$

#### Cos
$$\frac{d}{dx} (cos[f(x)]) = -(f'(x)sin[f(x)])$$
