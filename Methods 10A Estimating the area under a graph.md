## Vocab and definitions
### Left and point method 
$L_n = \frac{b-a}{n}[f(x_0)+f(x_1)+...+f(x_{n-1})]$
### Right end point method
$R_n \frac{b-a}{n} [f(x_1)+ f(x_2)+... + f(x_n)]$ 

### Trapezoidal rule 
$T_n = \frac{b-a}{2n}[f(x_0) + 2f(x_1)+...+2f(x_{n-1}) + f(x_n)]$
