## How can momentum be calculated
$\rho = mv$
Where 
- $\rho =$ $\textrm{momentum}(kgms^{-1})$ 

### Other Equations
- $\Delta \rho = \rho _{f} - \rho _{i}$ 

Consider $F=ma$ 
$F=\frac{mv-mu}{t}$ 
$F=\frac{\Delta \rho}{t}$
$\Delta \rho = ft$
