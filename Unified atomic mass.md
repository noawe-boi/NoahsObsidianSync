### What is an atomic mass unit?
One atomic mass unit ($1$u) is equal to $\frac{1}{12}$ the mass of a Carbon-12 atom (6 protons, 6 neutrons). 
	This equates to roughly the mass of a proton or neutron. 
#### Quantity
- $1.66054\cdot 10^-27$ kg
### Common masses
Neutron - $1.008665$ u
Proton - $1.007276$a u
