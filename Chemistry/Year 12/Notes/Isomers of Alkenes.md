### Properties of [[Alkene]] [[Isomer]]s
Considering straight chain [[Alkene]]s, there are more opportunities for [[Isomer]]s to appear.
#### Boiling points
Differs depending on what isomer is present
#### Density
Differs depending on what isomer is present
#### Example
$CH_3-CH_2-CH=CH_2 \;\neq\; CH_3-CH=CH-CH_3$
