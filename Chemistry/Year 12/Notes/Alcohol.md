### What are Alcohols?
Alcohols are a class of organic compounds that contains the [[Hydroxyl]] functional group. 
#### Naming alcohols
Find the main chain and ad '**ol**' as suffix that is immediately preceded by the locant.

#### Formula
$C_nH_{2n+1}+OH$
