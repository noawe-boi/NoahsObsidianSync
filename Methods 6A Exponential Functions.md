### Most logarithmic functions are in the form of

$y=a \times (b)^{x-c} + d$
#### $a$: Dilation in the y axis
	- will affect y int
- if negative $\implies$ reflects on the $x$ axis
#### $c$: Horizontal translation
- Horizonal translation 
	- $+c \implies$ left
	- $- c \implies right$ 
#### $d$: Vertical Shift\
- $+d$ $\implies$ up
- $-d \implies$ down
#### $x$: 
- if negative $\implies$ reflects $y$ axis
